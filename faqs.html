Ordering5 questions
How do I place an order on Agricom?▼
It's very easy! Browse our Products page, click any item to view details, choose your quantity, and click "Add to Cart". Once done, click the cart icon and proceed to checkout. You need to be logged in or create a free account to complete your order.
Do I need an account to buy from Agricom?▼
Yes, a free account is required. Creating one takes less than a minute — visit our sign-up page. With an account you can track orders, view purchase history, and save your delivery address.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?▼
You can modify or cancel within 2 hours of placing, as long as it hasn't been dispatched. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp at +250 780 000 000 or email support@agricom.rw. Once dispatched, cancellations are no longer possible.
Is there a minimum order amount?▼
No minimum order amount. However, orders below RWF 10,000 may attract a small delivery fee. Orders above RWF 50,000 qualify for Free Delivery anywhere in Rwanda.
Can I order in bulk for my cooperative or farm group?▼
Absolutely! We love supporting cooperatives. For bulk orders, contact us at hello@agricom.rw for a custom quote with discounted pricing and flexible delivery scheduling.

Delivery5 questions
Which areas do you deliver to in Rwanda?▼
We deliver to all five provinces:
  • Kigali City — next-day delivery
  • Northern, Southern, Eastern, Western Provinces — 2–4 business days
Contact us for very remote areas.
How long does delivery take?▼
Kigali: 1 business day. Other provinces: 2–4 business days. Times may be longer during peak planting seasons (March–May and September–November). You'll receive an SMS when your order is dispatched.
How much does delivery cost?▼
  • Orders over RWF 50,000 → Free Delivery
  • Kigali (under RWF 50,000) → RWF 2,000
  • Other provinces → RWF 3,000–6,000
The exact fee is shown at checkout before you confirm.
How do I track my order?▼
After dispatch, you'll get an SMS and email with a tracking number. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard. WhatsApp your order number to +250 780 000 000 for real-time updates.
What if my order arrives damaged or incomplete?▼
Photograph the damaged/missing items and contact us within 48 hours of delivery via email at support@agricom.rw or WhatsApp. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

Products5 questions
Are your seeds certified and genuine?▼
Yes! All seeds are certified by the Rwanda Agriculture and Animal Resources Development Board (RAB). Each package includes a certification label and lot number for full traceability.
Are your fertilizers safe for organic farming?▼
We offer both Organic and Chemical options, clearly labeled on each product. Our organic compost manure is fully safe for organic farming. Chemical fertilizers like Urea and NPK should be applied as per label instructions.
How do I know which seed variety is right for my area?▼
Each product page shows recommended altitude, rainfall zone, and soil type. General guide:
  • Highlands (1800m+): Irish potato, wheat
  • Midlands (1200–1800m): Maize, bean, sorghum
  • Lowlands (<1200m): Rice, cassava, sweet potato
Contact our agronomists for a personalized recommendation.
What is the shelf life of fertilizers and seeds?▼
Seeds: 12–18 months in a cool, dry place. Fertilizers: 2–3 years unopened; use within one planting season once opened. Always check the expiry date on the package.
Do you sell spare parts for farm tools?▼
Yes, for most tools we sell (sprayers, pumps, hoes), we can source replacement parts. Email support@agricom.rw with your product name and model number and we'll advise on availability and pricing.

Farming Advice5 questions
When is the best time to plant maize in Rwanda?▼
Rwanda has two main seasons:
  • Season A (September–February): Long rains — ideal for maize, beans, most field crops
  • Season B (March–August): Short rains — good for maize, vegetables, root crops
Plant in the first 2 weeks of the season once reliable rains have started for best yields.
How much fertilizer should I apply per acre?▼
For maize on 1 acre:
  • NPK 17-17-17: 50kg at planting
  • Urea (46% N): 25–50kg as top dressing, 4 weeks after germination
We strongly recommend a soil test first. Contact our agronomists for location-specific advice.
Do you provide agronomist support or farm visits?▼
Yes! We offer free phone and WhatsApp consultations for all Agricom customers. On-farm visits have a small facilitation fee depending on location. Contact us at hello@agricom.rw to schedule.
How do I control pests and diseases on my crops?▼
Integrated Pest Management (IPM) is best:
  • Use disease-resistant seed varieties
  • Practice crop rotation every season
  • Apply approved pesticides at recommended rates
  • Remove and destroy infected plant material
Send us a photo via WhatsApp and our agronomist will help identify the problem.
How can I improve my soil for better yields?▼
  • Add organic matter — compost manure, green manure, crop residues
  • Test pH and lime if acidic (most Rwandan soils are slightly acidic)
  • Practice minimum tillage to preserve soil structure
  • Use cover crops like mucuna in the off-season
  • Apply balanced fertilizers, not nitrogen alone
Our organic compost manure is a great starting point.
